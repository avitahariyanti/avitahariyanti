Nicholas Saputra

Young Talented Actor

Was born in Jakarta on 24 February 1984 from a German Father and Indonesian mother. Known in public after his first film debute in Ada Apa dengan Cinta? in the 2002 as Rangga, a cool and broken home highschool student. Nico was graduated from SMA Negeri 8 Jakarta and continued his study to University of Indonesia majoring Architecture.
Photography and travelling become his hobby, we can see some of his photograph at his instagram @nicsap. He always took pictures when he travelling around the world. We can see the world through his pictures. Besides in film he also took part as male model and VJ in the begining of his career. Here is some of his achievement in film:
